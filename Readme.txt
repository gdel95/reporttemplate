A Collection of LaTeX templates that I use every day.

It is nothing special but people may find them useful.

Feel free to use them for non-commercial purposes.

--------------------------------------------------
Note: I highly recommend that you install the texlive-full version. Many of the templates here are using packages that are not installed with the standard texlive version.
--------------------------------------------------

Templates in the "Classic" folders are the standard templates provided by LaTeX with no modifications. All "Classic" Templates should compile with a standard LaTeX installation without a problem.

Templates in the "KOMA Script" folders use the KOMA Script package: http://www.ctan.org/tex-archive/macros/latex/contrib/koma-script/

The rest of the folders contain templates that have been altered a bit by me. Most of the times these are the templates that I actually use.
